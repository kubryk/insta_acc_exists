 <p style="text-align: left;">Fulani braids are a great way to style your hair. So, if you are searching for daring yet beautiful braided hairstyles, look no more, here they are!<!--more--></p>

<p style="text-align: left;">Let’s start with the question: what are Fulani braids? It is a distinctive hairstyle that is widely spread in African communities and named after the Fula ethnic group. It is characterized by exceptional patterns of thin or medium tightly plaited braids that stick closely to the head.</p>

<p style="text-align: left;">Many women worldwide prefer this authentic hairstyle as a way to express themselves. Alicia Keys, for example, is one of the all-time braids lovers among celebrities. Besides, different styles of this type often involve colorful extensions and various decorative elements, for instance, beads, rings, and chains.</p>
